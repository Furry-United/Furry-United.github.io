Our story.

I was 11, all tired and scared on a scary rainy day. That was the day I never forgot. Step step step I heard them, walking toward me. I was sitting on a bench, it was soggy, covered in white liquid. I look up, but it was too late. "SNATCH!" "GRAB HIM GRAB HIM!" "ARE YOU ASSUMING HIS GENDER?!?" words thrown all over, unknown to me their meanings. I was covered in a bag, my senses stolen from me. Finally, theres a hole, I stick my fist through it, and make it bigger. I can finally see what was happening. Three big people in animal suits? No. Furry suits. I heard about this on the radio. People were getting kidnapped left and right, coming back as furry cubs and other. I was forced into a car. It was the only thing I could do. Run? From 3 big wolfs? I stood no chance, they were too big for me. I don't remember what happened next, but the faces I will never forget. I was forever traumitized.
